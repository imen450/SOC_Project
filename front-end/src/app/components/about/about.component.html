The collections are the perfect alliance between Parisian glamor and Italian know-how. The story behind the brand's must-have red sole harks back to the flawless creativity and flamboyant personality of the designer who, while working in his studio in the 1990s, borrowed a bottle of nail polish from his assistant Sarah and started painting the bottom of one of her shoes.
